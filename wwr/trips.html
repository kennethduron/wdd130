What to Bring

Bring a towel, a change of clothes, closed-toe water shoes, sunscreen, and a positive attitude. We provide lifejackets, helmets, and paddles.

Safety First

All trips are led by certified guides who will walk you through safety procedures before each departure. We also provide on-river support and first-aid trained staff.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes — cancellations and rescheduling are allowed up to 48 hours before your trip with a full refund. See our full policy on the Contact page.

Are trips appropriate for children?

We offer family-friendly beginner trips for children 6 and up. Check each trip card for minimum age requirements.
